Phase Description and Deliverables

 Phase 4  Description  Example of Deliverables
 Clinical & Pre-production

 In this phase, validation and clinical studies will be performed to ensure product design, safety and efficacy.

Activities such as design refinement, manufacturing process implementation and shelf-life testing are completed.

Inventory for launch may be built in parallel with this stage if pre-approved.

If clinical studies are not needed, Phases 4 & 5 may be combined pending approval.

  • Manufacturing Process Development, Tooling & Test Equipment
  • Complete equipment IQ/OQ/PQ
