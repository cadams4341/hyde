Phoenix Ambulatory Blood Pressure Monitor Project
Sub-project: Sphygmochron Spreadsheet

Project Description

The purpose of the Spreadsheet Project is to help out the Halberg Chronobiology Center by updating their Sphygmochron spreadsheet with fixes and features.  While doing so, we discover, develop, and record various bits of knowledge for later use in other Phoenix Ambulatory Blood Pressure Monitor projects.
