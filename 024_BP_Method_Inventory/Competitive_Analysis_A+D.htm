A&D Ambulatory Blood Pressure Monitor TM-2430

The TM-2430 is not so much a competing device as a representation of the current norm for ambulatory blood pressure monitors, particularly for chronobiology. The device is currently used by the Halberg Chronobiology Lab.

A&D Ambulatory Blood Pressure Monitor TM-2430
(Credit: A&D Company, Limited)

Description provided by A&D:

  1. Oscillometric measuring method
  2. Automatic measurement interval settable to 5, 10, 15, 20, 30, 60, or 120 minutes
  3. Stores up to 350 measurements (7+ days @ 30 minutes per measurement)
  4. Uses 3 "AA" batteries -- alkaline or Ni-Cd/Ni-MH
  5. Battery life is approximately 200 measurements (4+ days @ 30 minutes per measurement)
  6. Connects to:
    • PC through RS-232C cable

The TM-2430 is targeted for the clinical market. A&D's devices for the personal market do not support the automatic measuring needed for ambulatory blood pressure analysis.
