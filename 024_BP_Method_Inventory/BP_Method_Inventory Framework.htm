Phoenix Ambulatory Blood Pressure Monitor

Analysis Framework of Blood Pressure Measurement Methods

 

Suggested Taxonomy of Blood Pressure Monitors

  • sensor site and physiology -- what the device senses and why a signal would correlate to blood pressure
  • sensor type
  • form factor -- device geometry
  • mathematics
  • analytic technique -- transforming sensed signals into blood pressure measurements
  • patient constraints -- expectations on the person wearing the device
  • user constraints -- expectations on the person taking the blood pressure readings
