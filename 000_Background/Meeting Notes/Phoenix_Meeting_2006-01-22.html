Discussion

  • Larry Beatty. Fixed several bugs in the xchron, needs to complete testing, and identified more bugs and wants to discuss them with Germaine before he determines how to proceed. Germaine described that there are 20 clusters of users worldwide, each with several hundred individuals. The Sphygmochron is a prototype application. Chris Fuller asked if they had HIPAA clearance. Germaine said they have HIPAA and other clearances.
  • Gerry presented a clinical information systems minitopic the difference between an engineers and a physicians perpspective on diagnosis, where the physician uses the SOAP approach: subjective, objective, assessment and plan. When a physician iterates, they go back to objective, but typically not the subjective step, instead referring to another physician if this doesn't work.
