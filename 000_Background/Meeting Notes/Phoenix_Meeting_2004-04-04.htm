Phoenix Ambulatory Blood Pressure Monitor Project
4/4/2004 Meeting Notes

 

Attendees
Wade Peterson
Othilde
Franz
Jeff Rabel
El Nolley

Discussion

Reviewed the Website and email list server

Wade reviewed his prototype monitor using a peizo film sensor using the postings from website . He showed his prototype, showed the data, and showed that from the data we could measure heartbeat, blood velocity and blood pressure. With a modification using piezo cable with embedded weights, it could provide motion of the body.

Wade will experiment more, with more sensors, with piezo cable, different orientations.

We discussed fund raising to help support activities like building prototypes. Get corporate sponsors.
