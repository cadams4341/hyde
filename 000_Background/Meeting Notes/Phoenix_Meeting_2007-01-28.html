Sphygmochron Vision - Larry Beaty

 

Questions of:
* Is the code public domain or private.
* Franz seems to feel that the real value is the patient database. What do we do with it and how?

Chris noted that keeping the patient identities out of the "product" makes it possible to easily satisfy the regulatory requirement.

Larry talked about the requirements changing as Franz and Germaine saw what could be done in a program. We discussed capturing what they do now, and automating it as "version 1". If the patient data goes into a text file, Version-1 could be constrained to do that; if Germaine has to retrieve other measurements for this patient from another text file, it could include this as an automated function.
