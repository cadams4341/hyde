Meeting Parking

 

We meet at the University of Minnesota, Mayo 1250, on the 12th floor.

Parking is available at:

  • East River Road meters (bring about $4.50 in quarters for 3 hrs): http://www1.umn.edu/twincities/maps/Mayo/
  • East River Road Garage: http://www1.umn.edu/twincities/maps/ERivRdGar/
  • Washington Ave Ramp: http://www1.umn.edu/twincities/maps/WashRamp/
  • Weisman Art Museum Garage: http://www1.umn.edu/twincities/maps/WeisGar/
  • Church Street meters.
  • 4th Street Ramp: Free parking on Sundays except on special events: http://www1.umn.edu/twincities/maps/4StRamp/ about 0.8 mile from Mayo.
Campus Maps: http://campusmaps.umn.edu/tc/
